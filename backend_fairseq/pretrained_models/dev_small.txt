Helthl k'uuhl siwadi'm ahl la'oo'y g̲o'ohl Hla Heen.
Ky'ulhl k̲'ay 'mesim hanak̲' wat as Wiladoo ant 'wahl ky'eegwihl t'a'wihlg̲an.
Ii sii hlguuhlxwt loot iit haboo'lt.
Needii sim g̲al 'wudin 'nekwt ii k̲'ap g̲ala'y tusthl bent.
Ii sildim wiltxwst.
Ha'ondii 'nekwt dii sgyethl hlgu tk'ihlxw hlguuhlxw k̲'ay 'mesim hanak̲'si,
ii ap gyatt wilt gya'adiit, k̲'ap hogyag̲am didils wilt gya'ahl gyet.
Ii helt ant neediit wilaaxt wil 'nit hlguuhlxum t'a'wihlg̲an k̲'ay 'mesim hanak̲'si.
Ii lukw'il ky'aa 'wii 'nekwt.
G̲asg̲oohl hla 'wii t'ishl t'a'wihlg̲an tunsi.
Ii belgi gwitkw'ootxwhl enx̲ g̲anhl g̲ax̲biist g̲anhl huwilp.
Wihl ap gupgupxwidiihl wilt, wilt gya'ahl hli gyedihl ts'epsi, iit liksgyedinhl hli gyedihl g̲alts'epsi.
Iit seex̲diithl k'i'yhl wilp x̲hliphetxwit ahl hla k̲'aphl ts'epsi.
Iit dok̲s dipunhl g̲ax̲biist iit liiluxwdiit.
K'i'yhl sa iit nax̲'nis dipun wil yukwhl yook̲xwhl similoo'o.
Hets'im x̲jaax̲xwhl nax̲'nidiit.
